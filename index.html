International Student Services Office

The University of Batangas (UB) successfully navigates towards the path of acquiring a greater number of international students and providing opportunities for international collaboration, making UB as a home away from home. Strengthening the university's thrust in this aspect is the International Student Services Office (ISSO). ISSO supports the students' success by providing them with information about immigration issues, assisting them with academic and cultural adjustment, and creating opportunities for involvement in the UB community. ISSO further advances intercultural engagement through student exchange programs (both inbound and outbound) and provides culturally-sensitive activities throughout the campus.
